Tomáš Nagy

Research

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science of the Jagiellonian University, working with Marcin Kozik. I obtained my PhD degree in October 2023 at the TU Wien under the supervision of Michael Pinsker. I am interested in applications of universal algebra to the problems from theoretical computer science, in particular to the constraint satisfaction problem over infinite domains.
